About Us

What is CampusFood?

Food is almost synonymous with education. CampusFood is a company that delivers delicious campus cafeteria food to people across campuses in South Africa. More than that we are about the people.
Our journey began as students at Mut who just were frustrated with physically getting food from the cafeteria on campus with no other option.
